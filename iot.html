Introduction to IoT 2025

MOOC | Seminar | Data Science for IoT

Understand and learn the fundamental concepts and key enablers of the Internet of Things and how to identify and design IoT applications.

Course Content

1. Introduction

Architecture, hardware design, and power management for IoT systems.

2. OS & Software

Memory, energy management, and scheduling for embedded devices.

3. Sensing Layer

Sensors, intelligence, and the sensing pipeline implementation.

4. Networking

Short/Long range wireless technologies and IoT protocols.

5. Data Management

Distributed databases, Big Data, and Distributed Ledger Technology.

6. Security & Privacy

Threats, computational privacy, and communication security.
